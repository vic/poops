What?
Poops is a simple tamagochi game, where people cares for other people.

Why?
Our goal is to experiment with anonymous, non-text interaction between distant people. And investigate how it can help to fight social issues like depression.

Poops, I did it again
Well, that's not a question, but yes, shit happens everyday.
Our goal is to help people having a bad day get a bit happier by knowing that someone in someplace cares and wants him well.
